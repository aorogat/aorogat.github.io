About Me

I am a recent Ph.D. graduate from Carleton University specializing in Question Answering over Knowledge Graphs (QAKG). My research focuses on bridging the gap between natural language queries and structured knowledge through innovative benchmark creation and intelligent query systems. I have extensive experience in both academic research and industry applications, having worked at Ericsson on knowledge graph construction, data integration, and retrieval-augmented generation systems.

My work has contributed significantly to the field through the creation of large-scale benchmark datasets and automated evaluation systems for QAKG. I am passionate about making knowledge graphs more accessible through natural language interfaces and advancing the intersection of NLP and semantic technologies.
